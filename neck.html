Neck Exercises

Neck Flexion

Start by sitting or standing with your back straight. Place your hands on your forehead and gently apply resistance as you push your head forward, tucking your chin towards your chest. Hold the position for a few seconds and then release. This exercise targets the muscles at the front of your neck.

Neck Extension

Sit or stand with your back straight and your hands resting on the back of your head. Apply gentle resistance as you push your head backward, looking up towards the ceiling. Hold the position for a few seconds and then release. This exercise targets the muscles at the back of your neck.
